Amadeus Commands - Lesson & Quiz

Lesson: Basic Amadeus Commands

  • RT - Retrieve PNR (by record locator)
  • AN10DECMNLHKG - Availability for Manila → Hong Kong, Dec 10
  • AN10DEC MNLHKG/AA - Availability on specific airline
  • SS1Y1 - Sell 1 seat in Y class, line 1 of availability
  • SS2M2 - Sell 2 seats in M class, line 2
  • NM1PELAGIO/RONIAN MR - Add passenger name
  • NM2PELAGIO/RONIAN MR/KYLA MRS - Add 2 names
  • APM-09171234567 - Add phone contact
  • APE-email@gmail.com - Add email contact
  • TKTL/10SEP - Ticketing Time Limit
  • TKOK - Ticketing OK, ready to issue
  • ER - End and Save PNR
  • RF RONIAN - Received from (who made booking)
